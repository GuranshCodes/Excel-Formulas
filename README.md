<h2>About</h2>
<p>The Excel Invoice System is an automated, beginner friendly tool designed to simplify billing processes. By combining Excel formulas, dynamic dropdowns, and VBA automation, this system handles product selection, quantity conversions, and tax calculations automatically.</p>

<h2>Setup Quick-Start</h2>
<ol>
  <li>Create a new <strong>Excel Macro-Enabled Workbook (.xlsm)</strong>.</li>
  <li>Create two sheets: <strong>Invoice</strong> and <strong>Products</strong>.</li>
  <li>Populate your product list in the <strong>Products</strong> sheet.</li>
  <li>Use <strong>Data Validation</strong> to create dropdowns in your invoice table.</li>
  <li>Press <strong>Alt + F11</strong> to open the VBA Editor and paste the provided automation script.</li>
</ol>
